Why FAQ Pages Are a Priority?

FAQ pages continue to be a priority area for SEO and digital marketing professionals. An FAQ page is one of the simplest ways to improve your site and help site visitors and users. In no small part, the importance of FAQ pages has been driven in recent years by the growth in voice search, mobile search, and personal/home assistants and speakers.
